Key Focus Areas

Our platform highlights critical sectors in Ethiopia where digital transformation can create significant impact:

  • Healthcare: Streamlining patient care through centralized health record systems, telemedicine, and digital health services.
  • Education: Enabling efficient student management, national curriculum alignment, and enhanced e-learning platforms for schools.
  • Residential Property Management: Improving tenant and landlord communication through digital platforms for rent, maintenance, and incident reporting.
  • Skilled Labor Marketplace: Creating a digital marketplace for connecting skilled professionals with customers in need of services such as plumbing, electrical work, and more.
  • Transportation: Offering easy, digital ticketing and payment systems for long-distance travel between cities.
  • Real Estate: Providing verified property listings, secure transactions, and real estate advisory services to buyers and sellers.
  • Delivery Services: Supporting e-commerce growth with reliable and efficient last-mile delivery platforms.
